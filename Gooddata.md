**GoodData** is a cloud-based **analytics and business intelligence (BI) platform** that helps companies embed dashboards, reporting, and data insights into their products or internal systems.

Here’s a structured overview:

---

## 1. What GoodData Does

GoodData provides:

- 📊 **Dashboards & Reporting**
    
- 📈 **Data Visualization**
    
- 🧮 **Semantic data modeling**
    
- 🔌 **Embedded analytics for SaaS products**
    
- ☁️ Cloud-native deployment (multi-tenant ready)
    

It’s commonly used by SaaS companies that want to **embed analytics directly inside their application**.

---

## 2. Key Features

### 🔹 Headless BI & Composable Architecture

GoodData positions itself as a **headless BI platform**, meaning:

- The analytics engine is separated from the frontend
    
- You can use their UI components or build your own using APIs/SDKs
    

This is useful for engineering teams that want full control over UX.

---

### 🔹 Semantic Layer

One of their strongest features:

- Centralized **semantic data model**
    
- Metrics and business logic defined once
    
- Reusable across dashboards and apps
    

Helps maintain consistency in KPIs across teams.

---

### 🔹 Embedded Analytics

- White-label dashboards
    
- Multi-tenant analytics
    
- Secure data isolation per customer
    
- Designed for SaaS platforms
    

---

### 🔹 Deployment Options

- **GoodData Cloud** (managed SaaS)
    
- **Self-hosted**
    
- **Kubernetes-native deployment**
    
- Works with Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, PostgreSQL, etc.
    

---

## 3. Typical Use Cases
